# Chapter 3: Building Reliable Data Pipelines at Scale

## Overview

Data pipelines are the backbone of modern analytics and AI systems. Poorly designed pipelines create delays, errors, and trust issues across the business.

## Core Topics

- ETL vs ELT strategies
- Workflow orchestration
- Idempotent processing
- Retry and recovery patterns
- Dependency management
- Pipeline observability
- SLA driven delivery

## Key Principles

- Design for failure
- Automate operations
- Monitor everything
- Keep systems maintainable

## Key Takeaway

Reliable pipelines turn raw data into dependable business value at scale.
