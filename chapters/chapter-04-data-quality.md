# Chapter 4: Data Quality as a First Class System

## Overview

Data quality is essential for analytics, reporting, and machine learning. Bad data creates poor decisions, failed models, and loss of trust.

## Core Topics

- Completeness and accuracy
- Consistency and timeliness
- Validation frameworks
- Quality SLAs
- Monitoring and alerting
- Incident response
- Quality in ML pipelines

## Key Principles

- Measure quality continuously
- Prevent errors early
- Treat trust as a product
- Automate controls

## Key Takeaway

High quality data is the foundation of reliable business intelligence and AI outcomes.
