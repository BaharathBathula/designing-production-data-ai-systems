# Chapter 10: Governance and Compliance Deep Dive

## Overview

Highly regulated industries require strong controls over data privacy, access, lineage, and retention. Compliance must be operationalized through engineering practices.

## Core Topics

- GDPR requirements
- HIPAA controls
- Audit readiness
- Data retention policies
- Access logging
- Lineage tracking
- Breach response planning

## Key Principles

- Build controls early
- Automate evidence collection
- Protect sensitive data
- Reduce regulatory risk

## Key Takeaway

Compliance becomes scalable when it is embedded into systems instead of handled manually.
